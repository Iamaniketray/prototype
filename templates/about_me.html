Biography

Dr. Ranjeet Kumar Rout is a faculty member of the Information Technology Department at the Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT) Jalandhar, Punjab, prior to this institute he was working as Assistant Professor of CSE Department National Institute of Technology Srinagar, Hazratbal-190006, Srinagar, J&K. He had joined NIT Srinagar in 2018 and NIT Jalandhar in 2024. He received his Ph.D. (Engineering) from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST), Shibpur, West Bengal, and working place was at Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata, West Bengal. Dr. Rout has also worked as research personnel at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata where he worked on a project named “Understanding Genes and Genomes through Fractal and Mathematical Morphology”. The intent of this project was to provide valuable information about genomics and proteomics. Dr. Rout research interests include machine learning, deep learning, visual cryptography, Affective Computing and computational biology. He holds three patents (2- Indian and 1-USA) and has published more than 45 papers in peer-reviewed international and scientific journals in the field of non-linear Boolean functions, Computational biology and affective computing. He has supervised 02 (two) PhD students (+3 submitted and +1 Ongoing), He has three (03) research project(Ongoing) in the area of Agriculture-AI and CV etc. Currently, Dr. Rout has completed two (02) books “Artificial Intelligence Technologies for Computational Biology” and another book entitled “Advancement of Deep Learning and its Applications in Object Detection and Recognition”. He has also actively involved with different programs initiated by government of India as Nodal Officer, Ek Bharat Shretha Bharat (EBSB), Yuva Sangam- III and Yuva Sangam-IV. He is a lifelong member of the Computer Society of India (CSI), ISTE, member of IEEE and adjunct faculty of Centre of Excellence Energy Research (CEER) at NIT Srinagar.
