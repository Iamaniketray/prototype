Biography

Dr. Ranjeet Kumar Rout is a faculty member of the Information Technology Department at the Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT) Jalandhar, Punjab. Prior to this, he worked as an Assistant Professor in the CSE Department at NIT Srinagar, Hazratbal-190006, Srinagar, J&K. He joined NIT Srinagar in 2018 and NIT Jalandhar in 2024. He received his Ph.D. (Engineering) from IIEST Shibpur, West Bengal. His research was carried out at Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata. Dr. Rout has also worked on a project titled “Understanding Genes and Genomes through Fractal and Mathematical Morphology” at ISI Kolkata. His research interests include machine learning, deep learning, visual cryptography, affective computing, and computational biology. He holds three patents (2 Indian and 1 USA) and has published over 45 papers in peer-reviewed journals in the areas of non-linear Boolean functions, computational biology, and affective computing. He has supervised 2 PhD students (+3 submitted, +1 ongoing), and currently has 3 ongoing research projects in the area of Agriculture-AI and CV. He has completed two books: “Artificial Intelligence Technologies for Computational Biology” and “Advancement of Deep Learning and its Applications in Object Detection and Recognition”. Dr. Rout is actively involved in various government initiatives as Nodal Officer for Ek Bharat Shretha Bharat (EBSB), Yuva Sangam III and IV. He is a lifelong member of CSI, ISTE, IEEE, and an adjunct faculty at the Centre of Excellence Energy Research (CEER), NIT Srinagar.
